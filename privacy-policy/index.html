DATA WE COLLECT
Per Server
When the bot joins a Discord server, we save a unique identifier (ID) for server-specific settings, such as determining which commands can be used in certain channels.

Per User
When a user first interacts with the bot's internal economy system, we store another unique identifier, allowing us to store balances, prediction records, and similar data.

WHAT WE DO WITH YOUR DATA
We do not and will not share any collected information with anyone unless legally obligated to do so.

DATA RETENTION
We will retain all collected data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected. If you wish to have your data removed, you may contact us through Discord using the link provided in the "Contact us" section below.
